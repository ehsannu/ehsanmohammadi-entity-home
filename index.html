Ehsan Mohammadi (احسان محمدی)

Ehsan Mohammadi is an Associate Professor of Information Science at the University of South Carolina and a faculty member of the School of Information Science. He leads the Hi Da (HiDa) Research Lab.

Research interests include human-centered AI, health informatics, data science, misinformation, and social media analytics, with publications in venues such as JAMA Network Open, Journal of Medical Internet Research, and Information Processing & Management.
